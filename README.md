# Modernisation Barriers in the GDR

## Overview
This repository contains code developed in the context of the research project  
“Modernisation Barriers in Economy and Science of the GDR (Mod-Block-DDR)”

The project examines the persistence of structural and institutional constraints originating in the GDR and their long-term impact on regional economic development.

## Research Context
Funded by the German Federal Ministry of Education and Research (BMBF), the project is conducted by an interdisciplinary consortium of universities and international partners, coordinated by the University of Bremen.

## Contents and personal contributions to the project
- Digitisation of archival documents
- Data cleaning and structuring in R
- Preparation of datasets for empirical analysis

## Data 
The underlying data is based on digitised archival sources and cannot be shared publicly. 
The repository therefore focuses on the data preparation and cleaning pipeline.
